ASSASSIN

Hayabusa

Hayabusa is a high-mobility Assassin in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang renowned for his split-pushing, slippery escape mechanics, and single-target burst damage. He is a Jungler who excels at killing isolating enemies, executing squishy carries, and diving in and out of team fights.

Gusion

Gusion is a high-burst, melee Assassin in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, widely known for his lightning-fast combos, high mobility, and extreme carry potential. He excels at instantly eliminating isolated squishy targets, making him a dominant threat as a Jungler and the Mid Laner.

Ling

Ling is a highly-mobile Assassin in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang known for his ability to leap across walls. He excels at split-pushing, diving into the enemy backline to eliminate squishy targets, and escaping sticky situations. However, he is squishy, highly dependent on energy, and heavily countered by crowd control.

FIGHTER

Terizla

Terizla is a formidable Fighter in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, known for his exceptional innate durability, massive AoE burst damage, and reliable crowd control. Commonly played in the EXP Lane, he excels at frontlining during team fights and punishing enemies who group up with his ultimate.

Sora

Sora is a highly versatile Fighter/Assassin hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang known for his shifting cloud mechanics. His ultimate allows him to switch between an aggressive Assassin form (Thunder) and a durable Tank form (Torrent), allowing him to adapt his playstyle on the battlefield.

Paquito

Paquito is a highly aggressive, high-skill Fighter in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang. He is often played in the EXP lane, he excels at burst damage, mobility, and outplaying enemies through rapid skill combinations. Because he uses no mana, he relies entirely on his passive ability to stack and enhance skills.

MAGE

Luo Yi

Luo Yi is a highly impactful Mage in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang. She specializes in explosive AoE burst damage, continuous crowd control, and global map rotation with her ultimate, making her an important asset in both team fights and solo kills.

Cecilion

Cecilion is an infinitely scaling Mage in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang who dominates the late game. He is known for his massive mana-based burst damage and AoE poke. He excels at controlling team fights from the backline but is highly vulnerable to assassins due to his low mobility.

Valir

Valir is a specialized control and damage-over-time Mage in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang who excels at zoning enemies, and countering tank-heavy or short-ranged team compositions.

MARKSMAN

Layla

Layla is a classic Marksman in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, known for her explosive late-game damage and exceptional attack range. She excels at safely shredding enemies and destroying turrets from a distance, though she requires careful positioning due to her lack of escape skills.

Miya

Miya is a Marksman in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, known for her straightforward mechanics and excellent late-game scaling. She operates as a hyper-carry in the Gold Lane, relying on attack speed, critical damage, and multi-target basic attacks to shred enemy teams.

Hanabi

Hanabi is a popular Gold Lane Marksman in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, renowned for her devastating, bouncing AoE basic attacks and unique crowd-control immunity. She excels in sustained team fights but requires careful positioning due to her lack of dashes or escape skills.

SUPPORT

Angela

Angela is a top-tier utility Support hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Ban, known for her global map presence and reliable heals. She is best known for her Ultimate, which allows her to instantly teleport onto and shield any ally on the map, turning the tide of clutch team fights.

Floryn

Floryn is a dedicated Support/Heal hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang famous for her global map healing and unique item-sharing mechanic. She acts as a powerful protector who sustains the team through prolonged fights and gives a crucial advantage to the primary damage dealer in the team.

Estes

Estes is a pure Support hero or healer in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang who excels as a Roamer. He possesses some of the strongest AoE sustained healing capabilities in the game, making him important in team fight compositions.

TANK

Belerick

Belerick is a pure Tank hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang who primarily plays in the Roam or EXP Lane. He is widely known as the ultimate anti-Marksman frontline hero due to his ability to automatically counter-attack aggressors and lock down entire enemy teams with massive AoE taunts.

Minotaur

Minotaur is a hybrid Tank/Support hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang who operates primarily as a Roamer. He stands out as one of the few crowd-control and heavy frontline tanks who can simultaneously heal his teammates. He is a dominant pick for team-fight initiation, counter-engagements, and extended objective zoning.

Johnson

Johnson is a unique hybrid Tank/Support hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang who plays almost exclusively as an aggressive, high-tempo Roamer. He is famous for his ability to transform into a car and transport an allied hero across the map, creating deadly global ganking pairs and great team-fight initiations.
