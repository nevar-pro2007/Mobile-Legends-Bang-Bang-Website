OVERVIEW

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game and it is one of the most popular games around the world. Players can use different heroes, learn their skills, and develop strategies. They could battle in teams, kill enemies, and destroy the enemy base to win.

META HEROES

Harley

He is a high-mobility, single-target burst Assassin/Mage in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang who excels as a Jungler. He specializes in a "hit-and-run" playstyle, using teleportation tricks to blink onto squishy enemy heroes, kill them down with an explosive curse, and safely retreat before the enemy team can react.

Harith

Harith is a highly elusive and hyper-carry Mage in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, primarily played in the Gold Lane or as a Jungler. Known for his incredible sustained magic DPS and shield generation. He dominates team fights by dashing around, slowing enemies, and unleashing enhanced basic attacks.

Suyou

Suyou is a highly versatile hybrid Assassin/Fighter in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang renowned for his unique dual-form mechanic. By choosing to either tap or hold his skills, players can instantly switch between an agile, fast-paced Mortal form and a durable, hard-hitting Immortal form, granting him access to six different abilities.
